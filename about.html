Our Story

Premeo Sports INC

Premeo Sports Inc. USA, in association with its German office & Pakistani factory, is pleased to offer quality padel rackets, pickleball rackets, and FIFA-licensed soccer balls for end consumers in the United States of America, Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Qatar, and Pakistan.

Our Values

Uncompromising Quality: Every pickleball paddle, racket, and ball is rigorously tested for durability, balance, and performance to meet the highest industry standards.

Innovation & Technology: We utilize cutting-edge technology to design high-performance gear with lightweight materials, ergonomic grips, and precision-cut edges for superior gameplay.

Customer-Centric Approach: Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer secure shopping, fast shipping, and expert support to ensure a hassle-free experience.
